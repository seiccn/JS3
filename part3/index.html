Company

Your CV is your balance sheet- let us do the accounting for you.

"If you review a company's accounts, and see that they have a strong balance sheet, you are more likely to invest in that company than if they have a weak one.", Mrs Moneypenny, rewarded Financial Times columnist. Your CV is your first chance you get to make a good impression on a potential employer. A top-quality CV will considerably boost your chance of getting face-to-face interviews, thus it is worth spending time and effort on the content and presentation. It will make all the difference in obtaining the position you want. You must therefore highlight your skills, expertise and value. The majority of resumes sent to potential employers, employment agencies and recruitment agencies by jobseekers tend to be a word document resume and all of them are very close in structure and format.
